    Return Policy

    When you shop Nike online, either in the Nike App or on Nike.com,
    you can return items that are unworn and unwashed and still have their product tags attached within 30 days of purchase (some exceptions apply).
    That includes custom Nike By You sneakers.

    How To Return Order

    1. Sign in to your Nike Member profile and go to your order history. Select "View or Manage" for the order you're returning, then select "Start Return". If you purchased as a guest, you'll need to access your order using your order number and email address.
    2. Choose the items to return, provide the return reason from the dropdown menu, and select "Continue".
    3. Submit your return—we'll automatically email a printable pre-paid label. You can also choose to download the label.
    4. Pack your items for delivery and securely attach your printed return delivery label. Be sure to seal the package and remove or cover any previous delivery labels and barcodes to avoid delivery delays.
    5. Hand your return to the agent at the appointed time and location. Unattended returns won't be collected—someone must be present to hand over the return

    What Are Nike's Delivery Options

    During checkout, we'll provide an estimated delivery date. We offer the following delivery options:

    Standard Delivery

    • Free for orders of R 5,200 or more
    • R 250 for orders less than R 5,200
    • Generally arrives in 4-13 business days

    Deliver To A Location And Pick-Up

    Have your eligible order delivered (via standard delivery) to a convenient pick-up location.
